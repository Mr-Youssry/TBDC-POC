What's new in v3

International VC tab

Each company now has a dedicated international VC list covering non-Canadian investors — primarily US, European, and MENA funds — with geographic rationale and a specific outreach hook per fund. These are not generic "global VCs" — each is matched against the company's actual investor thesis, stage, and geographic profile.

Pre-conditions checklist

Every match now includes a pre-conditions checklist: what the company needs to have in place before the introduction is made. Categorized as Foundational (must-have), Timing (wait until X), or Framing (how to position). An introduction made before these conditions are met is worse than no introduction — it wastes a warm relationship.

Warm path bonus tab

A separate tab documents where Ahmed Korayem can personally activate a meaningful meeting — warm paths within 2 weeks, cold outreaches with a specific conversion angle, and honest acknowledgment of where TBDC's institutional relationships are the right tool rather than a personal network claim.

Activation next steps

Every Tier 1 and Tier 2 match now includes a specific next step — not a status field, but a protocol: who to contact, what framing to lead with, what to hold back, and what sequence to follow. A match score is not an outcome. It is the beginning of a workflow.
